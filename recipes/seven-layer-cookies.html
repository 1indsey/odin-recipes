7 Layer Cookies

No-fuss, easy-to-bake treat that everyone will love

These delicious 7 layer cookies have been a Christmas Eve staple in my family for generations. With just 7 ingredients and little prep-work, adding them to your dessert platter is a no-brainer. (And don't let the coconut and nuts scare you - even the pickiest of eaters that don't usually like these ingredients tend to still love these cookies!)

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cube butter
  • 2.5 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup finely shredded coconut
  • 6 oz chocolate chips
  • 6 oz butterscotch chips
  • 1 can Eagle brand condensed milk
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Melt ¾ cube butter in 9x13 pan.
  2. Add 2 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs and press together
  3. Sprinkle 1 C. fine coconut
  4. Sprinkle 1 6 oz package chocolate chips
  5. Sprinkle 1 6 oz package butterscotch chips
  6. Press down with hands
  7. Drizzle condensed milk
  8. Sprinkle chopped nuts over that and press down
  9. Bake 30-35 minutes at 325 degrees Fahrenheit
  10. Cut in squares once cooled
